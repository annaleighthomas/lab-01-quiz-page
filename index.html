The Smiths

The Smiths were a English band formed in Manchester in 1982. The band consisted of singer Morrissey, guitarist Johnny Marr, bassist Andy Rourke, and drummer Mike Joyce.
The Smiths were signed to the independent label Rough Trade Records. The Smiths wrote four full lenght records self-titled record The Smiths in 1984, "Meat Is Murder" in 1985, The Queen Is Dead" in 1986, and "Strangeways, Here We Come" in 1987.
Internal tensions led to their break up in 1987. They are the greatest band of all time.

The Smiths

The Smiths were a English band formed in Manchester in 1982.
The band consisted of:

  • Morrissey, singer
  • Johnny Marr, guitarist
  • Andy Rourke, bassist
  • Mike Joyce, drummer
The Smiths were signed to the independent label Rough Trade Records.
The Smiths wrote four full lenght records:

  1. "The Smiths" self-titled (1984)
  2. "Meat Is Murder" (1985)
  3. "The Queen Is Dead" (1986)
  4. "Strangeways, Here We Come" (1987)
Internal tensions led to their break up in 1987.
